## Mouse Trap Car

For this school project I challenged myself with compound levers, allowing for much more string to be pulled from the same mouse trap.  
It worked up until the competition, where it did not perform as expected.

## Slight Issue

With most of the weight on one side, the wheels had uneven grip.  
This made the car turn slightly as it moved.  
This was never fixed and we just rotated the car at the starting line.
